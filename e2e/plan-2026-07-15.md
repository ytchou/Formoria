# E2E plan — submission enrichment lifecycle

## Changed journey

Admin submission review now derives its stage from the latest submission target and parent curation job. Enrichment persists on the submission, and approval creates the brand.

## Coverage

- Seed a pending submission and assert it appears under `待資料處理` with no brand row.
- Seed a future curation job for that submission and assert polling moves it to `抓取中` with no brand row.
- Complete the target with complete `enriched_data` and a staged image, then assert it moves to `可供審核` with no brand row.
- Approve from the admin UI and assert the RPC creates one approved brand, links the submission, and promotes the staged image.

## Verification

- `pnpm exec playwright test e2e/tests/admin-submission-enrichment.spec.ts --project=deep --list`
- Run the spec against an environment with both 2026-07-15 migrations applied before merge/deploy smoke verification.
